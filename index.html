Plants with Purpose

Plants with Purpose is a Young Enterprise Company from Hurstpierpoint College in West Sussex. Our planting kits include a range of herbs to choose from, including Thyme, Lavender, Mint, Chamomile and Rosemary.

Included in your plant kit is a ceramic pot, a bag of soil and a mini chalkboard with chalk to display a personal message – all packaged in a reusable fabric bag. Our informational leaflet provides a potting guide and tips to keep your plant healthy, along with details of its many uses, whether for food, for self-care or for your home.

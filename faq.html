Frequently Asked Questions

What does Plants with Purpose sell?

We sell a plant-growing kit, including a premium ceramic pot, soil, a chalkboard label with chalk, and the plant itself. Every plant is unique and that is why we also include a useful leaflet which provides a thorough explanation of the plant’s purposes and creative uses, ranging from aesthetic to culinary - it also acts as an easy guide on how to grow and maintain the plant.

Where can I purchase a plant kit?

Press 'Buy' in the menu at the top of the page - from there you will be able to select your plant kit and taken to the YE Trading Station where your payment will be processed.

To purchase your plant kit, press 'Add to basket', ‘View Basket’ and then ‘Checkout’, where you will be asked to fill in billing/shipping information and can review your order. Once you have reviewed your order, simply press 'Complete Order' and you are done.

Where are our plants from?

All our plants are sourced locally from Tate's South Downs Nurseries and Garden Centre in Hassocks.

How often should I water my plant?

You should water your plant 1-2 times a week, or if the soil feels dry to the touch.

Why can't I pay with credit card?

Unfortunately as we are a Young Enterprise corporation, and our team are under 18 years of age, we are legally restricted from accepting credit card payment. However, our website makes it easy to pay securely by bank transfer.
